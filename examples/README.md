# Quantum Nexus

**A quantum realm exploration framework by n3xion**

Quantum Nexus is a comprehensive Python framework for exploring quantum mechanics, quantum physics, and advanced quantum phenomena including axiom keys, entanglement simulations, and morphing life systems.

## Core Modules

### 1. Axiom Key
Fundamental quantum principles and axioms that govern the quantum realm.

### 2. Quantum Mechanics & Physics
Core quantum operations, wave functions, and physical simulations.

### 3. Doppelganger Entrapments
Quantum entanglement simulations and dual-state phenomena.

### 4. Morphing Life
Dynamic quantum state transformations and adaptive quantum systems.
